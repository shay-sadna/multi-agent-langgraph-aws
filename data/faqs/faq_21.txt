Q: What ETFs are available on Leumi Trade?
A: Leumi Trade offers hundreds of ETFs across TASE (Teudot Sal) and US exchanges. Popular categories include TA-125 index trackers, S&P 500 ETFs, bond ETFs, sector-specific ETFs (tech, healthcare, energy), and commodity ETFs (gold, oil). Use the "ETF Screener" under "Research" to filter by category, risk level, and expense ratio.
